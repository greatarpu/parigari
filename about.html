Our Story

Serving Humanity with Faith Since 2004

Established in 2004

Parigari Social Welfare Trust was founded with a comprehensive vision: to provide a safety net for the most vulnerable members of our society. Our work spans across Old Age Homes, Orphanages, Educational support, and Rural Development.

Our Purpose

We are dedicated to providing relief to the downtrodden, the differently-abled, and women and children facing poverty. Our bottom line has always been clear: WE ARE IN SERVICE FOR THE PEOPLE.

In rural areas like Thiruvottiyur and Ennore, we have pioneered women’s empowerment by conducting free tailoring schools, turning skill-building into a path toward financial independence.

Our Philosophy

"Our workers often serve under high-risk conditions and with limited economic resources. However, we move forward with unwavering faith—depending on the One who never forgets to feed even the birds."

While government facilities are often few or obsolete, Parigari Trust steps in as an urgent response to the critical needs of Health, Education, and Women’s Upliftment.
